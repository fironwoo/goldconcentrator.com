## Original project problem

Document the customer's actual problem here: clay-bound feed, uncontrolled boulders, poor classification, fine-gold loss, unstable water supply or a previous plant that did not match the material.

## Evidence collected

List only information that can be supported:

- Representative feed photographs and sampling method
- Target and achieved feed rate
- Size distribution and visible gold observations
- Assay or test-work references
- Approved process and equipment records

## Why the selected route was used

Explain the alternatives considered and why the final washing, classification and recovery stages matched the ore. Do not present the selected equipment as a universal alluvial gold package.

## Results

Publish recovery, concentrate grade, production or commissioning data only when the measurement method and operating conditions are documented. Otherwise describe the delivered scope and the next verification step.
