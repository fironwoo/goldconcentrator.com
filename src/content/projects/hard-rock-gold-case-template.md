## Ore and project context

Record the ore type, weathering, known mineral association, representative head grade, top size, target throughput and project constraints. Avoid describing a single rich hand specimen as representative ore.

## Test and design question

State what the team needed to learn. Examples include gravity-recoverable gold, target grind size, circulating-load behavior or whether a sulphide-bearing fraction required external flotation or leaching tests.

## Flowsheet decision

Describe the crushing stages, grinding and classification logic, gravity recovery point and the treatment planned for gravity tailings. Explain which data caused the route to change.

## Verifiable outcome

Attach approved photographs, test reports, equipment records and commissioning notes. Numerical performance belongs here only after the source and operating period have been checked.
